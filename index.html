WEEK 8: INJURY UPDATE
Here are the top injuries to report from this week and the players who will likely benifit from the injuries. Starting at the quarterback position, it appears that QB Alex smith will sit out week 9 with a concussion which leaves backup, Nick Foles, at the helm of the Chiefs offense. Also, it appers that Steelers QB, Ben Roethlisberger will return this week after sitting out week 7 and having his bye week 8. In the running back department, it appears DeMarco Murray is nursing a toe injury so watch for rookie, Heisman trophy winner Derrick Henry to pick up some of the workload. Charcandrick West also looks to have a big week due to the fact that Jamaal Charles has been placed on Injured Reserve and fill-in Spencer Ware will most likely sit out of Week Nine's contest against a weak Jacksonville run defenese with a concussion. In the wide reciever department look for slot reciever, Randall Cobb to return to action against a struggling Colts secondary. Some of the leagues top recievers such as Amari Cooper, Larry Fitzgerald, T.Y. Hilton, and Odell Beckham Jr. are all battling with injuries but they should all be good to go for week nine barring any significant setbacks.
